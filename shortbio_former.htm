Evžen Koèenda - Short Biography

Short Biography

    Graduated in 1985 from the Prague School of Economics in International Trade Management. M.A. in Economics from the University of Toledo, Ohio in 1992. Graduate studies in Economics at the University of Houston, Texas with a Ph.D. degree in 1996. 1996-1998 Deputy Director for Research at CERGE, Charles University, Prague and the Economics Institute of the Czech Academy of Sciences. 2002-2003 the Deputy Director for Graduate Studies at CERGE-EI. 1996-1999 Assistant Professor, 1999-2004 Associate Professor at CERGE, Charles University, Prague and a Researcher at Economics Institute of the Czech Academy of Sciences. Since 2004 Professor of Economics at Charles University in Prague. Current affiliation (since 2015): the Institute of Economic Studies, Charles University. Research Fellow of the CESifo (Munich) and IOS (Regensburg), and EABCN. Economic Advisor to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic in 1997. 1998-1999 a member of the Scientific Council of the Ministry of Transport and Communications. Member of the editorial boards of the Journal of Comparative Economics (2004-2006), Economic Systems (2009-2013). Co-editor of the Czech Journal of Finance and Economics (since 2014). Main areas of research include applied econometrics, international finance and European integration.

Even Shorter Biography

    Evzen Kocenda is Professor of Economics at the Institute of Economic Studies, Charles University in Prague. He received his Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Houston. He has taught at the Charles University, the New York University and the Universite Paris IX – Dauphin. Affiliated with the CESifo, IOS and EABCN. Member of the editorial boards of the Journal of Comparative Economics (2004-2006), Economic Systems (2009-2013). Co-editor of the Czech Journal of Finance and Economics (since 2014). Main areas of research include applied econometrics, international finance and European integration.
