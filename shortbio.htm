Evžen Koèenda - Short Biography

Short Biography

    Graduated in 1985 from the Prague School of Economics in International Trade Management. M.A. in Economics from the University of Toledo, Ohio in 1992. Graduate studies in Economics at the University of Houston, Texas with a Ph.D. degree in 1996. Since 2004 Full Professor of Economics. Current affiliation: Charles University in Prague, Institute of Economic Studies, Chair of the Department of Finance and Director of Graduate Studies. Research Fellow at the CESifo (Munich) and IOS (Regensburg), and EABCN. Expert assessment and consulting for European Commission, World Bank, CESifo, Czech National Bank, Czech Governement. Economic Advisor to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic in 1997. Member of the editorial boards of the Journal of Comparative Economics (2004-2006), Economic Systems (2009-2013), Emerging Markets Finance and Trade, and Comparative Economic Studies. Co-editor of the Czech Journal of Finance and Economics (since 2014). Main areas of research include applied econometrics, international finance and European integration.

Even Shorter Biography

    Evzen Kocenda is Professor of Economics at the Institute of Economic Studies, Charles University in Prague. He received his Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Houston. He has taught at the Charles University, the New York University and the Universite Paris IX – Dauphin. Affiliated with the CESifo, IOS and EABCN. Member of the editorial boards of the Journal of Comparative Economics (2004-2006), Economic Systems (2009-2013). Co-editor of the Czech Journal of Finance and Economics (since 2014). Main areas of research include applied econometrics, international finance and European integration.
